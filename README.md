--Swiggy Sales Data Analysis Dashboard--

A comprehensive data analytics project that processes close to 200,000 raw transactional rows from Swiggy using Microsoft Excel . The project cleans messy operational data and transforms it into a centralized, highly interactive visual dashboard to uncover key business insights.


🎯 Project Objectives
Analyze Ordering Patterns: 
Identify peak ordering hours, days, and months to optimize delivery partner schedules.
Understand Food Preferences: Compare Vegetarian vs. Non-Vegetarian revenue streams to guide restaurant onboarding.
Geographic Insights: Pinpoint top-performing Indian states and cities for targeted marketing budgets.


🛠️ Data Cleaning & Preparation
Scanned and removed duplicate entries for perfect data accuracy.
Handled missing customer feedback values to protect average rating metrics.
Standardized plain text dates into true Excel Date formats.
Created custom calculated columns for Day Names and Quarters using Excel formulas.


📊 Key Metrics & KPIs
Total Sales: ₹53.01 Million
Total Orders: 197.43K delivered dishes
Average Order Value (AOV): ₹268.51
Average Rating: 4.34 Stars
Ratings Count: 5.59 Million reviews


💡 Top Business Insights
The Veg Dominance: Vegetarian dishes are the clear winner, driving 63% (₹33.3M) of total revenue.
Bengaluru is the Core Engine: Bengaluru alone generates ₹5.5M in sales—outperforming Lucknow and New Delhi combined.
Weekend Surge: Saturdays experience the highest order volume (₹7.8M), while Mondays and Tuesdays are the slowest.
Consistent Quality: Customer satisfaction stays completely flat at a healthy 4.34 stars across all quarters.
